    Table Tennis
    Rules
    • The rules of the tournament of the International Table Tennis Federation as adopted from time to time by the Table Tennis Federation of India shall apply unless otherwise modified.
    • Matches will be held on the basis of five singles for boys and three singles for girls.
    • The order of play shall be as follows.
      • Three players on one side are numbered A, B and C and the three players on the other side are numbered X, Y and Z.
      • For girls:
          A v/s X        B v/s Y        C v/s Z       
      • For Boys:
          A v/s X        B v/s Y        C v/s Z        A v/s Y        B v/s X       
    • The result of the tie shall be declared when either of the team wins three matches. The number of players representing any college shall not be less than three or more than four for boys. Only four certificates shall be awarded to a winning team for boys. The number of players representing any college shall be three for girls. Only three certificates shall be awarded to a winning team for girls.
    • Each single shall be played on best of five games basis with each game of 11 points for boys and best of three for girls.

    Cricket
    " Any active sportsman has to be very focused; you've got to be in the right frame of mind. If your energy is diverted in various directions, you do not achieve the results. I need to know when to switch on and switch off: and the rest of the things happen around that. Cricket is in the foreground, the rest is in the background."
          
    Sachin Tendulkar
    Rules
    • Maximum 16 players are allowed in a team.
    • Tournament will be played on T-20 format.
    • Normal T-20 rules apply.
    • Tournament will be played on knock out format .
    • Tie in the game will be resolved by a super over.
    • Tie in case of points will be resolved by comparison of 'net runrates of all the matches' played by the team.
    • Teams should report 15 minutes before the scheduled time if the team is late by more than 5 minutes, the match would be forfeited to the opposition team.
    • All the teams should come in whites.
    • Team has to complete its quota of overs in 1hour 30 minutes.
    • If the 1st bowling team fails to complete then they will be penalized and have to chase the score in the number of overs they have bowled in the specified time.
    • If the 2nd bowling team fails to complete then runs equal to (current run rate of the batting team * the no of overs left) will be added to the batting side total,and also 2nd bowling team will have to bowl the overs left.
    • Decision of Umpires and Organizing committee shall be final and binding.

  • Lawn Tennis

    close
    Lawn Tennis
    Rules
    • The match shall be played on the best of three sets. The match will be played on the Davis Cup lines, i.e in the sequence two singles ,one double and two reverse singles.
      The two singles players on one side are named A&B and two singles players on the other side are named X&Y.
      The order of play shall be:
            A vs. X       B vs. Y       Doubles       A vs. Y       B vs. X
      Player playing at A, B, X and Y will be submitted by the captain before the start of the match.
    • If tie exists during the reverse singles, both the reverse singles will have to be completed.
    • The results of the full match shall be declared when either of the team wins three matches.
      The tennis Tie Break Rules shall be applied in all the sets(7/12 points).
      Tie will be resolved by successfully applying the following criteria.
      i) Individual Matches won/Individual Matches lost.
      ii) Sets won by the Team/Sets lost by the team.
      iii) Points for/Points Against-set score to be taken.
    • The score standing ,when the match is decide,shall be considered for the calculations indicated above.
    • The number of players representing an institute/college shall not be less than 2 or more than 4. Not more than four certificates shall be awarded to a team.
    • The general rules for tennis (women) are the same.The sequence followed would be first singles,one doubles and second singles.As in boys,the selected players for the matches will be submitted by captain before the start of the match.

    Basketball
    Rules
    • All matches will be of 40 min duration with 4 quarters of 10 min each. There will be a 10 min break after half time and two 2 min breaks after every quarter.
    • A team can take maximum of 3 time-outs in every quarter with 2 min duration each.
    • A team can take maximum of 3 time-outs in every quarter with 2 min duration each.
    • Teams must be present 10 min before the scheduled time. If the team is arriving 10 min later than the scheduled time, the match will be considered as a walkover.
    • Referees decision will be final.
    • Dress code:
      All players must wear shorts, and vests. The shoes must be soft sole sports shoes. Playing barefoot or in formal shoes, chappals, sandals etc. is not allowed. This is to ensure that injuries do not take place due to inappropriate equipment.
